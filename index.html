Places I have been.

  • Kansas City, KS
  • Detroit, MI
  • Branson, MO

Kansas City, KS

Kansas City, Kansas is the first place I have been on vacation, and also the place I have been too the most. This state is very close to my heart because it is where most of my family on my dad's side is from. I first met my Grandfather and some of my cousins on my first trip there when I was very young.

Detroit, MI

Detroit is the second place I have been to on vacation, also when I was very young. this is where most of my family on my mom's side used to live. I went to visit my Great Grandmother, once when I was born, a second time when I was around three years old and a final time for her funeral a few years ago.

Branson, MO

Branson, Missouri, is one of the best places I have been too. I have only gone once, again, when I was pretty young. We visited the beach a few times and saw a Stage preformance there. I don't remember many things about that time but what I do remember was lots of fun.
